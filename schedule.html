Notes

  • Muslim Quarter Stalls: For items like Grilled Lamb Skewers and Spicy Lamb Face Salad, the best experience is often at the many famous small stalls in the Muslim Quarter (Huimin Jie) that specialize in a single item.
  • Local Street Vendor/Shop: For common breakfast items like Hulatang or Hot and Sour Dumplings, look for small, busy shops or street vendors near your hotel or a major intersection for the most authentic taste.
  • Restaurant Names: he names in parentheses are suggestions for well-known or famous restaurants for that particular dish, based on reputation and popularity in Xi'an. Xi'an has many restaurants that serve various traditional dishes. Ask locals for recommendations to find the best ones.
